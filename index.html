About Metanova 2026

Metanova 2025 is an 8-hour hackathon designed to bring together innovators, developers, and problem-solvers under one roof. It focuses on open innovation, medical technologies, and creative problem-solving through coding and design. The event aims to promote collaborative learning and real-time technical application among students by exposing them to real-world challenges and hands-on experience.
